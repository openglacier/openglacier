# Roadmap

> *The roadmap describes where the initiative is going, not when it will arrive.*

The evolution of openglacier is guided by long-term objectives rather than release dates.

Priorities may evolve.

Principles do not.

---

# Phase 1 — Foundations

Establish the common language of the ecosystem.

Objectives include:

- defining the architecture
- defining the object model
- documenting the initiative
- establishing shared vocabulary
- designing interoperability
- building trust through transparency

Success means everyone understands the vision.

---

# Phase 2 — Core Ecosystem

Build the foundations that allow resources to communicate.

Objectives include:

- object lifecycle
- event system
- identities
- resources
- agents
- missions
- communication between components

Success means isolated technologies become a connected ecosystem.

---

# Phase 3 — Workspace

Create a workspace capable of representing the ecosystem.

Objectives include:

- object visualization
- contextual interaction
- zoom-based navigation
- responsive experience
- accessibility
- calm interfaces

Success means people understand their ecosystem naturally.

---

# Phase 4 — Intelligence

Enable collaboration between individuals, agents and artificial intelligence.

Objectives include:

- autonomous agents
- mission orchestration
- explainable automation
- collaborative intelligence
- contextual assistance

Success means intelligence emerges through communication.

---

# Phase 5 — Sustainability

Extend the lifetime and value of digital ecosystems.

Objectives include:

- support for legacy hardware
- efficient resource usage
- local-first capabilities
- interoperability with existing technologies
- measurable digital sustainability

Success means technology creates less waste while delivering more value.

---

# Phase 6 — Community

Grow an ecosystem of contributors, organizations and adopters.

Objectives include:

- documentation
- education
- governance
- open collaboration
- shared best practices

Success means the initiative becomes larger than its original creators.

---

# Long-term vision

The ultimate objective is not to create another software platform.

It is to contribute to a healthier digital ecosystem.

An ecosystem where:

- technology deserves trust
- communication creates intelligence
- sustainability is built into design
- individuals remain free to choose
- organizations trust people

If these ideas continue to spread, the initiative has already succeeded.
