# Branding

> *A brand is not what it says. It is what people consistently experience.*

## More than a name

openglacier is not a company.

It is not a product.

It is not a platform.

It is an initiative that promotes a healthier, more transparent and more sustainable digital ecosystem.

Every project carrying the openglacier name should reflect these values.

---

## A promise

The openglacier name represents a commitment.

A commitment to:

- openness
- transparency
- interoperability
- sustainability
- simplicity
- trust

Technology changes.

These commitments do not.

---

## The meaning of the name

The name combines two complementary ideas.

**Open** represents:

- open standards
- open ecosystems
- open communication
- open collaboration
- open source

**Glacier** represents the health of our planet.

Like glaciers, digital ecosystems can slowly deteriorate without anyone noticing.

Technology should contribute to preserving both.

---

## Trust is the brand

Trust is not created through marketing.

It is earned through consistent behavior.

Every decision.

Every interface.

Every interaction.

Every line of code.

Should reinforce trust.

---

## Simplicity is respect

People should never feel excluded because technology is unnecessarily complex.

Simplicity is not the absence of capability.

It is the absence of unnecessary complexity.

---

## Neutrality

openglacier does not promote specific vendors, providers or technologies.

The ecosystem remains open.

Individuals choose.

Organizations choose.

Freedom requires neutrality.

---

## Sustainability

Technology should extend the life of existing devices whenever possible.

Replacing hardware should never be the default solution.

Digital sustainability is part of environmental sustainability.

---

## Transparency

Technology should explain itself.

People should understand:

- what happens
- why it happens
- who is responsible
- what changed

Transparency creates confidence.

---

## Consistency

The openglacier experience should feel familiar across every project.

Concepts remain consistent.

Vocabulary remains consistent.

Behavior remains consistent.

Consistency builds confidence.

---

## Humility

openglacier does not aim to replace everything.

It aims to connect what already exists.

The ecosystem is more important than any individual technology.

---

## A long-term vision

The initiative is designed to outlive technologies.

Success is not measured by adoption alone.

Success is measured by influence.

If the principles of openglacier inspire better digital ecosystems—even without using openglacier itself—the initiative has already succeeded.

---

## A recognizable feeling

People may forget the logo.

They may forget the website.

They may even forget the name.

But they should remember how it felt.

Simple.

Honest.

Transparent.

Calm.

Trustworthy.

That is the openglacier brand.
